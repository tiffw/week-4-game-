Crystal Game

Welcome to Crystals!

Here's the scoop: The goal of the game is for your Number to be the same as the Match number. To add value to your number click a crystal. Each crystal has a random number assigned to it.

  1. Click a crystal to get started
  2. Jot down the value of that crystal you just clicked by seeing the total for that crystal being added to your number.
  3. Click another crystal and remember the number associated that crystal. Then repeat to get the total of the Match number. Good Luck!!
